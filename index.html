Eligibility & Restrictions

All entrants must 18 or older and a Canadian citizen. Successful entrants must also be currently working in or near the town of Blue Mountain. Please be ready to provide proof of identity and residency upon entry. Please be advised all finalists will be contacted through the listed business contact information above. All applications must be entered prior to the deadline of August 9th 2017.

Prizes

Our committee of judges will carefully evaluate all applications and select four finalists. These finalists will then be given a final competition to determine our grand prize winner. Our lucky winner will receive one weeks accomodations for themselves and a guest at the luxorious Fairmont Southampton, in the beautiful Bahamas, as well as a state of the art Bose Home Entertainment System. Best of luck to all entrants!

About Us

Blue Mountain is a community built entirely around the tourism industry. The most important part of that has always been and will always be the employee. After consulting with many leading local businesses we have decided to give back in the best way we can.. Consider this competition as our token of thanks for all the wonderful employees who help make this community what it is today!
